How to Use Manual Lead Generation
  1. Copy the prompt below to your clipboard
  2. Open your preferred AI engine (ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude, etc.)
  3. Paste the prompt and run it
  4. Copy the AI's response (the JSON results)
  5. Paste the results below and click "Process Results"
  6. Review and select leads to add to your CRM
